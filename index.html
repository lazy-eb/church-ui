Reflection

The apostle Paul struggled to keep the eternal faith that God had started in him. He was preserved the faith so that he was able to speak boldly, and from now on the crown of righteousness is laid up for me. The Lord, the righteous Judge, will give it to me in that day. Can we say on like this? Think it. Let us leave the worldly faith go towards God and labor to keep the faith, the salvation of our soul and continue the run boldly like Paul. Let us receive the crown of life that the Lord has prepared for us.

I have fought the good fight, I have finished the race, I have kept the faith.
